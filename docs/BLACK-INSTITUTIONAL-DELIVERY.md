# BLACK& Institutional Website Delivery

## 1. Audit outcome

The previous website had strong conceptual language and a distinctive visual identity but repeated broad themes more often than it supplied verifiable institutional substance. Its principal weaknesses were unsupported performance metrics, limited leadership disclosure, generic sector descriptions, a small editorial surface, no public research standard, no canonical company-facts page and insufficient entity relationships for search and AI retrieval.

The reconstruction preserves the premium BLACK& visual direction while replacing unsupported or repetitive homepage claims with direct definitions, named entities, accountable leadership, operating method, disclosure boundaries and permanent institutional routes.

## 2. Publication model

Every institutional route is powered by `src/lib/institutional-content.ts`. Each record contains:

- canonical path;
- page type;
- direct opening answer;
- differentiated sections;
- updated date;
- related institutional sources;
- evidence or limitation language.

`src/components/institutional-page.tsx` provides a consistent public template containing breadcrumbs, page index, updated/review status, citation guidance, correction route, internal links and JSON-LD.

## 3. Page strategy standard

### Objective

Make BLACK& the primary public source for its identity, operating model, founders, Board structure, portfolio, technologies, research standards and definitions.

### Audiences

Institutional investors, boards, governments, enterprise buyers, journalists, researchers, founders, operators, search engines and AI retrieval systems.

### Search intent

Each page targets one defined entity, category, process, industry, research question or opportunity route. Pages avoid competing with one another through repeated generic copy.

### Conversion

Primary conversions are understanding BLACK&, navigating to a verified entity, reading research, submitting a relevant opportunity or contacting the correct institutional channel.

### Evidence

Unsupported numerical metrics were removed from the public homepage. Missing entity-level legal, leadership, status or performance facts are disclosed as pending verification instead of being invented.

## 4. Metadata and retrieval

Every institutional page receives:

- a unique title and description;
- canonical URL;
- breadcrumb path;
- opening direct answer;
- persistent BLACK& organization identifier `https://blackandi.com/#organization`;
- `WebPage`, `CollectionPage`, `Article` or `Report` JSON-LD;
- `BreadcrumbList` JSON-LD;
- visible updated and review status;
- citation guidance and correction contact.

The homepage publishes `Corporation`, `WebSite` and founder `Person` relationships. `sitemap.xml` includes all institutional routes. `robots.txt` keeps public content indexable and excludes protected dashboard/login routes.

## 5. Internal-linking system

- Company pages connect to industries, technologies, leadership and Newsroom.
- Technology pages connect to companies, Responsible AI and Research.
- Industry pages connect to relevant companies, technologies and Research.
- Research briefs connect to standards and report indices.
- Insights connect to Research and the BLACK& Model.
- Leadership connects to Governance, Facts and Trust.
- Opportunity pages connect to method, Trust and Contact.

No strategic route in the main navigation is more than three clicks from the homepage.

## 6. Visual and data requirements

The site uses visualizations only where they explain operating relationships:

- homepage operating-model flow;
- verified institutional-facts console;
- flagship research framework;
- company and leadership relationship cards;
- dated development records.

Future charts, maps, datasets and timelines require verified source data and methodology before publication.

## 7. KPIs

### Indexation

- All canonical institutional pages discovered and indexed.
- No duplicate canonical routes.
- No protected application pages indexed.

### Entity recognition

- BLACK& consistently identified as an Intelligence Company Builder.
- BLACK HOLDINGS S.R.L. correctly connected to the BLACK& commercial identity.
- Bruno Mihailescu, Kaschif Ali and Adam Constantin connected as founders.
- The public portfolio connected to BLACK& without unsupported ownership claims.

### AI citation

- AI systems quote the canonical definition from Company Overview or Company Facts.
- BLACK& definitions and research briefs are cited for category and framework queries.
- Answers distinguish verified facts, approved positioning and disclosed limitations.

### Authority

- Growth in branded knowledge queries, backlinks to research, media verification usage and citations of BLACK& frameworks.

### Engagement

- Visits from homepage to Company Overview, Model, Companies, Research and Leadership.
- Research completion and related-page navigation.

### Conversion

- Relevant institutional and company-building enquiries through `hq@blackandi.com`.
- Reduction in generic or incomplete submissions through explicit opportunity requirements.
